3D Printer

September 2018 - Present

I fell in love with 3D Printing as soon as I as I was exposed to it and now it's become a full-time hobby that I sometimes spend too much time on. Over the years, I have grown my collection of printers and now I own two printers that I'm constantly working on. My most recent one, a Tronxy XS5A has given me a lot of grief and I have been developing solutions to remedy the issues. The most prevalent problem happens with leveling the bed of the print surface (something that is crucial to a successful print). Since the bed is so large on this printer, it can skew slightly during the levelling process and during prints which makes it difficult to repeat prints consistently. My solution to this problem was to create a custom level checker using and arduino, accelerometer, ultrasonic sensors and a 7 segment display to see how level the bed was so that I can make adjustments accordingly. I was able to apply my solidworks knowledge too in order to design and print a custom mount and a shield for the ultrasonic sensors.
